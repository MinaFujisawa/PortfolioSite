ABOUT

I made two brain training games, Low to High and Stroop Test. They were my very fist apps that I made from scratch.

CHALLANGE

Since I was very beginner at Android app development, main theme was learning basic concepts and get used to them. Furthermore, using CountDownTimer and switching layouts in the same activity were challenging.

#1 Low to High

Replication of one of the games from the video game console called Brain Age by Nintendo.

Numbers appear in boxes for a short period of time. Afterwards, you must touch the boxes from lowest number to highest by memorizing the numbers.

If you select a wrong number twice, you lose.

Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puyo_Puyo#Gameplay

#2 Stroop Test

One of five words (blue, black, yellow, green and red) will appear on screen, one after another, each appearing in a random color (which may not match the color denoted by the word).

The player must choose the color of the word, rather than its semantic meaning (e.g., if the word BLUE appears in red, the player should select "RED").
